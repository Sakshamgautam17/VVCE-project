Sample MCQ Questions
1. What is the capital of India?

A) Arsh
B) Sapandeep
C) Delhi
D) Rome
Answer: C) Delhi

2. Which planet is known as the 'Red Planet'?

A) Venus
B) Mars
C) Jupiter
D) Saturn
Answer: B) Mars

3. In which year did World War II end?

A) 1941
B) 1945
C) 1950
D) 1963
Answer: B) 1945

4. Who is known as the "Father of Computers"?

A) Alan Turing
B) Charles Babbage
C) Isaac Newton
D) Albert Einstein
Answer: B) Charles Babbage

5. What is the chemical symbol for water?

A) H2O
B) O2
C) CO2
D) HO
Answer: A) H2O
